1.Uyire En Uyire

Uyire En Uyire" is a Tamil song from the album "Poojai", released in 2014, composed by Yuvan Shankar Raja and sung by him as well.
Here's a more detailed breakdown:
Song Title: "Uyire En Uyire" (meaning "My Life, My Life").
Year of Release: 2014.
Music Composer: Yuvan Shankar Raja.
Singer: Yuvan Shankar Raja.
Where to Find: You can find the song on JioSaavn and Gaana apps.

2.Paapa Paattu

"Paapa Paattu" is a song from the Tamil film "Veetla Vishesham", sung by Sid Sriram with music composed by Girishh Gopalakrishnan, and lyrics written by PA Vijay.
Here's a more detailed breakdown:
Song Title: "Veetla Vishesham".
Starring: The movie stars RJ Balaji, Sathyaraj, Urvashi, and Aparna Balamurali.
Music: The music for the song is composed by Girishh Gopalakrishnan.
Singer: The song is sung by Sid Sriram.
Release: The song was released on June 7, 2022.
